Who's Lana del Rey?

Lana Del Rey, whose real name is Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. She gained significant recognition and popularity for her unique vocal style, nostalgic and cinematic aesthetics, and her introspective and melancholic songwriting. Del Rey's music is often described as a blend of pop, indie, and alternative genres, with influences ranging from 1950s and 1960s Americana to modern-day pop music.
